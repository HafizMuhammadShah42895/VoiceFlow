Why We Built VoiceFlow

Typing long emails or documents by hand is slow and exhausting. While traditional dictation software exists, it is often clunky, requires you to dictate into a separate window, manually copy-paste the text, and painstakingly fix grammatical errors by hand.

We built VoiceFlow to eliminate this friction entirely. VoiceFlow is a frictionless, OS-level integration. There is no window to switch to. You simply hold a hotkey, speak, and perfectly formatted text is typed directly into whatever app you are currently using—whether that's Word, Chrome, or your IDE.

The Benefits

🚀

Unmatched Speed

By typing at the speed of speech and utilizing AI to instantly rewrite your thoughts, you can cut your writing time in half. VoiceFlow eliminates the delay between thinking and creating.

✨

Guaranteed Professionalism

Never send an email with a typo again. The Llama-3 AI acts as your personal editor, instantly restructuring messy thoughts into clear, professional, and grammatically perfect English.

🔒

Total Privacy

The core speech-to-text transcription is powered by a locally-hosted AI model (Faster-Whisper). Your voice is processed directly on your computer's hardware, meaning your private conversations are never uploaded to the cloud.

Global Hotkeys

Works inside any app. Press Alt+Shift to speak, and VoiceFlow types directly into Word, Chrome, ChatGPT, or your IDE.

Floating UI Overlay

Never wonder if it's listening. A sleek overlay stays on top of your screen to show real-time processing status.

Selection-Based AI Polish

Highlight any messy text and press Ctrl+Shift. Llama-3 rewrites your grammar instantly at the speed of light.

Custom Prompts

Take full control of the AI. Write your own custom system prompt to instruct the AI exactly how it should format or rewrite your text.
