Zero-Trust Privacy Protocol

We believe your voice data belongs to you. VoiceFlow is architected for maximum security.

Locally Hosted Dictation

The core Whisper engine runs directly on your hardware. Your voice recordings never leave your device and are processed entirely offline.

Explicit AI Control

When using cloud AI, only the exact text you manually highlight is sent to the Groq API. Nothing else is ever transmitted.

100% Air-Gapped Mode

For ultimate enterprise security, enable Offline Mode. VoiceFlow will connect to your local Ollama instance, severing all cloud ties permanently.
