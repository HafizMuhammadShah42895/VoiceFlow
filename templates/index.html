📖 How to use VoiceFlow

1

Hold your Dictation Hotkey (default Alt+Shift) anywhere to speak. Release the keys to let VoiceFlow automatically paste the text into your active app.

2

Highlight any messy text and press your AI Feature Hotkey (default Ctrl+Shift) to instantly rewrite and fix it in place.

🔒 Your Privacy is Guaranteed

All of your audio is processed securely on your own system. The core dictation and transcription engine runs entirely locally, meaning your voice recordings and audio files never leave your device.

When you use the AI feature with Groq, only the specific text you highlight is sent to the Groq API for correction. If you require absolute zero-trust privacy, you can switch to Offline Mode (Local AI) and use Ollama (a completely open-source local model). With Ollama enabled, 100% of your data stays on your machine and nothing is ever sent to the cloud!
